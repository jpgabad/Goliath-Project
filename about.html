About Us

Who We Are

Goliath caters to customers worldwide and provides detailed HR and Project Management guidance.

Our business model is based on powering organisations through complex projects and helping manage HR responsibilities. With a greater focus on compliance in Project Management and Human Ressource Management, we provide dedicated resources and solutions for your business. With your guidance and our dedication, we help you master your projects and achieve top tier human resource management.

What We Do

Human Resource Management

Goliath assists firms with people-centric tasks necessary for corporate growth and development. Taking control of HR functions such as hiring and onboarding, payroll, consulting, compensation management, and more are part of this.

Goliath Operations HR Consultancy works with both small and large companies. Our services include consulting on employee benefits, satisfaction, workplace politics, work culture, building a diverse recruiting policy, selecting an insurance plan, and everything else HR-related.

Project Management

We oversee projects throughout their entirety; we cover planning, scheduling, monitoring, implementation and closure.

Our project managers at Goliath are tasked with performing all these roles and can effectively take charge of your project from start to finish. If it is more basic support you are looking for, we also offer a la carte services. This approach can help organisations achieve a secure end to their projects by reducing bottlenecks and other constraints.

Client Satisfaction

We strive to maintain and uphold our clients' trust and confidence they put in us. Many of our clients who have used our HR consultancy services will be able to vouch for the quality of service we provide, often surpassing their expectations. From developing employee benefits to implementing HR software in the organisation, we have worked on handling a diverse number of complex HR problems.
