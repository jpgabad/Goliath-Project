Our Services

We offer many services related to the Human Resource and Project Management domain. Our services cater to the requirements of organisations today and help create a plan for the future.

HR Services

Goliath will work to help both small and large organisations in their quest to maintain smooth HR operations. Goliath will cover all your business's needs, from helping to streamline HR processes for large organisations to providing small business owners with the immediate administration and managerial relief they need.

HR Policies

Goliath will optimise HR processes by devising policies for the employee handbook and setting guidelines for employee conduct in the workplace. We work as a fully functional branch of the HR department for organisations looking to manage employees while motivating them to focus on the business's short and long term goals.

HR Consultancy

Our Consultants have years of experience and know-how in developing HR strategies that align with your business objectives, delivering value to your company by discovering cost-cutting and process-simplification opportunities.

We use our years of experience, industry expertise, and a client-centric approach to provide relevant, practical, and cost-effective solutions to our client's challenges, including HR and organisational strategy needs.

Our HR consultancy services afford clients the luxury of focusing their attention on other revenue-generation initiatives while maintaining the trust and confidence of their employees through our flawless HR management services.

Project Management Services

To evolve and build your business, you must invest in its infrastructure, and the best method to do it is through well-defined projects. Such projects can get complicated, but working with a firm with a team of seasoned, highly skilled, and experienced outsourced Project Management experts who will ensure that your project is completed on time, on budget, and according to specifications can help limit that risk.

At Goliath, our Project Management experts facilitate completing your projects when you struggle to maintain an appropriate level of in-house personnel.

We believe that game-changing Project Management support services are at the heart of any successful organisation, and Goliath provides unique and customised Project Management services to ensure that your projects operate smoothly.

Our project managers go beyond their typical roles to plan, organise, lead, and control your project through the lifecycle.

Some of the other roles performed by the project manager include:

  • Assigning qualified specialists to different roles
  • Defining and formulating goals
  • Setting a communication plan with the customer
  • Defining areas of responsibility
  • Attending to contracts and agreements
  • Constant communication with the team
  • Tracking the project status
  • Attending to all needs of the team and project

Why Do You Need a Professional Project Manager?

You can't best utilise all of the developers, analysts, testers and engineers working in your team if there isn't a project manager leading their efforts. Communication is crucial for a successful project, and project managers act as a conduit for effective collaboration through their presence and guidance.

Our Project Managers have the following necessary skills to see your project to completion such as:

  • Team Lead
  • Product Owner
  • Scrum Master
  • Project Coordinator
  • Delivery Manager

We have experts who can help your business transform faster by providing Project Management services that make it simple to manage and maintain your organisational infrastructure and work environment. They take care of the support layers and remove the hassle of operations from the user's end, reducing the requirement to hire staff regularly to monitor it.

We develop KPIs with our clients and base our Project Management services on them. We also provide ongoing consulting services as needed throughout the project, gradually introduce process improvements, monitor and analyse their success, and make adjustments as needed.

Project Scheduling from Start to Finish

We collaborate closely with project teams to plan operations from the start of the project through its completion. Our teams monitor the following requirements:

Confirm Project Activities

We begin by assisting clients in comprehending the extent of the job. The project scope can assist organisations in their operations and provide a thorough action plan for the future. The project scope might also specify a series of actions and goals that must be met.

Throughout the process, our staff at Goliath is in close communication with stakeholders to ensure that the goals are met.

Determining Dependencies

We establish dependencies during the project scheduling process and identify interrelated tasks. Identifying dependencies has saved valuable time for our clients and helped us finish projects on time or earlier than expected.

Set Duration

We create a project schedule timeline with a well-defined start and end date. The timeline is set as per the client's expectations from us, and it paves a realistic plan for the future. The time allocated to each task is laid out for teams to follow so that monitoring and controlling can become more manageable.
